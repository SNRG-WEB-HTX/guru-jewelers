Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence
in Fine Jewelry

Guru Jewelers has been a trusted Houston institution since 1989. What began as a family passion for beautiful, honest jewelry has grown into one of Houston's most beloved fine jewelry destinations — located in the heart of Traders Village on N Eldridge Parkway.

We believe every customer deserves to feel comfortable, informed, and genuinely cared for. Whether you're choosing a diamond engagement ring, commissioning a one-of-a-kind custom piece, or bringing in a cherished heirloom for repair — we treat every piece as if it were our own.

Our Metals

Gold Purity Guide

We carry all gold grades to suit every style, preference, and budget.

10K
41.7% Pure Gold

The most durable gold option, ideal for everyday wear. Bright, beautiful, and budget-friendly. Perfect for rings and bracelets that take daily use.

14K
58.3% Pure Gold

The ideal balance of purity and durability. The most popular choice in the US for engagement rings and fine jewelry with lasting brilliance.

18K
75% Pure Gold

Rich, warm, and luxurious. 18K gold delivers the deepest golden hue — the preferred choice for high-end jewelry and platinum-level luxury pieces.
