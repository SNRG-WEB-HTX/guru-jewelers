Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence
in Fine Jewelry

Guru Jewelers has been a trusted Houston institution since 1989. What began as a family passion for beautiful, honest jewelry has grown into one of Houston's most beloved fine jewelry destinations — located in the heart of Traders Village on N Eldridge Parkway.

We believe every customer deserves to feel comfortable, informed, and genuinely cared for. Whether you're choosing a diamond engagement ring, commissioning a custom piece, or bringing in a cherished heirloom for repair — we treat every piece as if it were our own.
